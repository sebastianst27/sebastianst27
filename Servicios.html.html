APARTHEID? What do you think?

Apartheid was a system of racial segregation that took place in South Africa and Namibian until 1992, and in Rhodesia until 1979. In short, this system of racial segregation consisted of the creation of separate places, both for housing and for study or even recreation, for the different racial groups, in the exclusive power of the white race to exercise the vote and in the prohibition of marriages or even sexual relations between white and black as the data shows us.

Personal pronouns

Within personal pronouns, the English language distinguishes between subject pronouns and object pronouns. They are also known as subject or nominal personal pronouns. They are those that refer to grammatical persons. They are:

English= I You He She It They We You
Spanish= Yo Tú Él Ella Ello Ellos Nosotros Ustedes
